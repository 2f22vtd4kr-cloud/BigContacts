The critical gap
The app is excellent at telling you who a businessman is and what assets they own (when the ingestors have run). It has no answer for the last mile: how to actually reach them. The 102 ingested entities are names from beneficial ownership filings — they disclose that "PETER THIEL" holds >5% of company X, but not his email address or LinkedIn. Bridging that gap requires either: (1) a Companies House / LinkedIn / Lusha enrichment ingestor, or (2) an OSINT workflow for manually tracing contact vectors with the app as the workspace. The Business Engineer and Data Engineer personas both pointed directly at this, and the app currently has no answer for it.
