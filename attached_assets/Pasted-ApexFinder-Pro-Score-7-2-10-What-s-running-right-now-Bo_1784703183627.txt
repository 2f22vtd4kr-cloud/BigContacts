What's broken right now ❌
The single biggest gap — startup timing bug:
The relationship triggers fire at 15s/20s/25s/30s/35s after boot. But on a fresh DB, auto-ingestion takes 4–6 minutes to load 32,200 entities. All the relationship jobs fire on an empty table and produce zero edges. By the time data exists, the timers are long gone. Result:
* totalRelationships: 0 — graph shows one isolated green dot
* Network Graph is completely non-functional on every cold start
* MCTS can't traverse anything (no edges = no paths)
* Pipeline CRM stays empty
This isn't a design flaw — it's one sequencing bug. The triggers need to fire after ingestion completes, not at wall-clock offsets from boot.
	
	
	
	
	
	
The gap to 9/10
Three specific fixes close most of it:
1. Sequencing fix — instead of setTimeout(..., 15_000), have the auto-ingestion endpoint fire a POST /api/relationships/rebuild-allwebhook on completion. Everything downstream (MCTS, enrichers, persona loop) should chain off ingestion-complete, not boot-time.
2. Name normalization — EDGAR names arrive LAST FIRST ALL CAPS. A single toTitleCase()pass on ingest fixes "THIEL PETER" → "Peter Thiel".
3. Redis contact cache restore — Context.md says 115+ entries were in Upstash slot 2, but contactableCount: 0 here. The startup restore step isn't matching entities on this import. Either the key pattern changed or the entity IDs shifted.
Fix those three and the app jumps to 8.8–9.0. The pipeline architecture, UI, and data sourcing are already at that level — the runtime state just doesn't reflect it on a cold start.
